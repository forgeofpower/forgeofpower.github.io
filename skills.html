    Skills

    Skills describe types of actions your character might specialize in or out of combat.

    Name Description
    Arcana How much you know about arcane magic and creatures
    Athletics Perform deeds of physical prowess or grace
    Crafting Create, understand, and repair items or structures
    Intimidation Bend other to your will using threats or an imposing presence
    Nature Know about the natural world, and train animals and magical beasts
    Perception Spot, hear, or otherwise detect the presence of something
    Performance Use your talents to impress a crowd or make a living
    Persuasion Influence others through negotiation and flattery
    Religion Know secrets of deities, dogma, faith, and the realms of the divine
    Society Understand the people and systems that make civilization run
    Stealth Avoid detection, slip past foes, hide, or conceal an item
    Thievery Maneuver vehicles through difficult terrain and dodge enemy fire
    Wrestling Grapple, trip, disarm other creatures, and defend against the same

    Arcana

    Good with Intellect.

    Includes:

    • Knowledge about spells
    • Knowledge about magic creatures
    • Knowledge about magic items
    • Inventing spells

    Athletics

    Good with Strength and Agility.

    Includes:

    • Running
    • Climbing
    • Swimming
    • Jumping
    • Balancing

    Crafting

    Good with Intellect.

    Includes:

    • Crafting
    • Repairing
    • Alchemy
    • Construction
    • Appraising

    Intimidation

    Good with Strength and Spirit.

    Includes:

    • Threatening
    • Blackmailing
    • Imposing
    • Scaring
    • Taunting

    Nature

    Good with Intellect and Spirit.

    Includes:

    • Knowledge about plants
    • Knowledge about natural creatures
    • Navigating
    • Handling animals
    • Foraging

    Perception

    Good with Intellect and Spirit.

    Includes:

    • Seeing
    • Hearing
    • Reading emotions
    • Noticing lies
    • Finding clues

    Performance

    Good with any Agility and Spirit.

    Includes:

    • Singing
    • Dancing
    • Impressing
    • Party tricks
    • Inspiring

    Persuasion

    Good with Intellect and Spirit.

    Includes:

    • Convincing
    • Deceiving
    • Flattering
    • Negotiating
    • Speechwriting

    Religion

    Good with Intellect and Spirit.

    Includes:

    • Knowledge about faiths
    • Knowledge about planar creatures
    • Preaching
    • Converting
    • Moralizing

    Society

    Good with Intellect and Spirit.

    Includes:

    • Knowledge about cultures
    • Knowledge about history
    • Fitting in
    • Socializing
    • Networking

    Stealth

    Good with Agility and Spirit.

    Includes:

    • Hiding
    • Blending in
    • Disguising
    • Concealing objects
    • Obscuring tracks

    Thievery

    Good with Agility and Intellect.

    Includes:

    • Pickpocketing

    • Disarming traps

    Wrestling

    Good with Strength and Agility.

    Includes:

    • Grappling
    • Pushing

    • Disarming
