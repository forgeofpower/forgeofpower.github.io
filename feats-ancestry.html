    Ancestry Feats

    Name Ancestry Type Description

    Wild Sprint Awakened You drop down onto all fours to run at high speed
    Cousins Everywhere Dwarf You have an enourmous extended family, with relatives everywhere you go
    Dwarven Resilience Dwarf You have developed a natural resilience to common hazards
    Logic Purist Elf You hold to logic above emotion, strictly controlling your demeanor
    Mind Meld Elf You link minds with another creature, sharing experiences back and forth
    Razor Teeth Goblin You can chew your way through solid walls
    Underfoot Goblin You scurry beneath the feet of larger creatures
    Dubious Knowledge Human You recount a crazy fact that may or may not be true
    Natural Talent Human You have a genius talent in your particular field of study
    Nature Meld Kami You meld with your sacred feature, disappearing from the world
    Thoughts and Prayers Kami You absorb the prayers of nearby devotees to boost your own power

    Awakened

    Amphibious Awakened

    You can swim at your full Speed. You can breathe water as easily as air.

    Wild Sprint Awakened

    Duration
    1 minute

    Your Speed increases by 20 feet.

    Dwarf

    Cousins Everywhere Dwarf

      Daily
    Requirements
    You are near a group of 100 or more people

    You declare that one of your relatives is nearby. You can choose your relative’s general personality, temperament, and profession, subject to the GM’s approval. Your relative is friendly towards you and will help you with simple favors, but will not risk their life to help you.

    For example, if you are in a normal town, you can declare that your second cousin works as a blacksmith, and then go visit him to repair your sword. Or if you’re in the BBEG’s giant volcano lair, you can declare that your father’s brother-in-law’s niece works in the lair as one of the BBEG’s henchmen.

    Dwarven Resilience Dwarf

    You have 5 Poison Shield. Choose one of the following:

    • You have 5 Fire Shield
    • You have 5 Cold Shield
    • You have 5 Bludgeoning Shield

    Elf

    Logic Purist Elf

    You have a +5 bonus to your DC against Emotion attacks and against Notice rolls to read your intentions or emotions.

    You speak in a perfect monotone.

    Mind Meld Elf

      Attack Mental
    Target
    One creature you can reach

    Specify the kinds of experiences you’re trying to share between you and your target. For example, you could choose to share memories of a certain event, current experiences of emotions, or thoughts on philosophy. The GM determines which exact experiences of this type are shared.

    Make an Intellect attack roll against your target’s Intellect DC. (As with all rolls, you can choose to automatically fail or critically fail this roll.)

    Critical Success
    You learn two experiences from your target
    Success
    You learn two experiences from your target
    Critical Failure
    Your target learns one experience from you

    Goblin

    Razor Teeth Goblin

    Make an unarmed Strike. The base damage of this strike is 1d4. This strike deals an extra 20 damage against inanimate objects.

    You can efficiently tunnel through walls by repeatedly using this power. As a rough estimate, in one minute of work, you can tunnel through 1 foot of soft material (dirt, sand, plaster) or a few inches of hard material (wood, loose stones, old masonry). You can’t tunnel through materials you can’t damage, such as solid stone or metal.

    Underfoot Goblin

    You can move through other creature’s spaces at full speed, even spaces occupied by enemy creatures. You can hide behind creatures that are larger than you.

    Human

    Dubious Knowledge Human

      Daily Retroactive

    State a claim or rumor about the world that you have no evidence for but that might plausibly be true. The GM assigns a DC for this claim based on how extreme it is: a reasonble claim would be DC 5-10, a very unusual one might be DC 15, while a crazy or impossible claim would be DC 20 or more. The GM and the player should agree on the DC. Then, the GM rolls a d20 in secret. If this d20 meets or exceeds the DC, then the claim is actually true, otherwise, the claim is actually false.

    Natural Talent Human

    You learn one Novice talent of your choice. This talent must be from a class that you have at least one level in.

    Kami

    Nature Meld Kami

      Encounter Polymorph
    Target
    One of your sacred features you can see within 10 feet
    Duration
    Until your target falls to 0 health or you decide to end it

    You meld with your target, disappearing and entering your target’s space. When this effect ends, you reappear at a space of your choice within 10 feet of the part of your target you entered.

    Thoughts and Prayers Kami

      Daily

    You regain 2 Mana, plus 1 Mana for each devotee of your concept within 1 mile.

    Lizardfolk

    Dragon's Breath Lizardfolk

      Attack Encounter
    Area
    30-foot line

    When you first pick this feat, choose one of Acid, Cold, Electricity, Fire, or Poison. This power permanently gains that trait for you.

    Make a Strength attack roll against your target’s Agility DC.

    Critical Success
    Your target takes 4d8 + your character level damage
    Success
    Your target takes 2d8 + your character level damage
    Failure
    Half damage

    Lizard King Lizardfolk

    Your size increases by 1 and your reach increases by 5 feet.

    Orc

    Clan Axe Orc

    You have a clan axe, which has the stats of a bludgeon, a heavy weapon, or a throwing weapon. When you succeed on a Strike with your clan axe, you deal an extra 1 damage. You cannot be disarmed of your clan axe. If you ever lose your clan axe, you always somehow find it again the next day, even if your clan axe was seemingly stolen or destroyed. If you sell or willingly give up your clan axe, you permanently lose this power.

    Orc Drop Orc

      Daily
    Trigger
    You fall to 0 health

    You fall to 1 health instead.

    Outsider

    Construct Flesh Outsider

    You have 10 Piercing Shield.

    Sense Emotions Outsider

      Attack Emotion
    Range
    30 feet
    Requirements
    You haven’t used Sense Emotions against this target in the last 24 hours

    Make a Spirit+Notice attack roll against your target’s Spirit DC.

    Critical Success
    You learn all the major emotions your target is currently feeling, plus an emotional secret your target is hiding
    Success
    You learn all the major emotions your target is currently feeling

    Undead

    No Vital Organs Undead

    When a Physical attack critically succeeds against you, you take damage as if that attack had only been a success.

    Paralyzing Touch Undead

      Attack Encounter Necrotic
    Target
    One creature you can reach
    Duration
    Until the end of your next turn

    Make a Spirit attack roll against your target’s Spirit DC.

    Critical Success
    Your target is unconscious
    Success
    Your target is stunned
    Failure
    Your target is slowed
