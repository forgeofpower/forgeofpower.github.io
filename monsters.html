    Golems are human-shaped objects, like suits of armor or scarecrows, that have been enchanted to be able to walk.

      Construct Unintelligent
    Attributes
    Strength +1, Agilty +0, Intellect -2, Spirit -1
    Other stats
    Health 20, Defense 12, Speed 25 feet, Size +0
