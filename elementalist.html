    Earth Spikes Novice You cause a spike of earth to erupt from the ground beneath the enemy
    Elemental Blast Novice A blast of a particular element damages everyone in a line
    Elemental Shield Novice You surround yourself with a shield of a particular element

    Talents

    Affinity Novice

    Choose a damage type. You gain 20 Shield for the chosen trait. When you cast a spell with the chosen trait, you ignore all Shield your opponent has against that trait.

    Concealing Fog Novice

      Ritual Spell
    Cost
    1 Mana
    Range
    120 feet
    Area
    20-foot burst
    Duration
    1 hour, or until you decide to end it

    You conjure a cloud of dense fog that fills the area. The fog completely obscures vision, making sight impossible and nullifying Visual effects. The fog partially obscures sound, imposing disadvantage on Auditory attacks and die rolls to hear things within the area.

    Heightened

    For each +1 Mana, the radius of the burst increases by 20 feet.

    Earth Spikes Novice

      Attack Piercing Spell
    Cost
    1 Mana
    Range
    120 feet
    Area
    20-foot burst

    The ground in this spell’s area becomes different terrain, as spikes or pillars of earth cover the landscape. Make a Magic attack roll against your target’s Agility DC.

    Critical Success
    Double damage
    Success
    Your target takes 2d6 damage and falls prone
    Failure
    Half damage

    Heightened

    For each +1 Mana, the damage increases by 1d6 and the radius of the burst increases by 10 feet.

    Elemental Blast Novice

    Specializations

    Air

    You have advantage on the attack roll if your target is airborne. On a success, your target is knocked back 20 feet.

    Earth

    The damage dice are d12s instead of d10s. On a critical success, your target takes double damage.

    Fire

    This spell is Fire instead of Bludgeoning. The damage dice are d12s instead of d10s. On a failure, your target takes half damage.

    Water

    You have advantage on the attack roll if your target is underwater. On a success, your target is pushed 10 feet in any direction.

      Attack Bludgeoning Cantrip Specialize Spell
    Cost
    0 Mana
    Area
    30-foot line

    Make a Magic attack roll against your target’s Agility DC.

    Success
    Your target takes 1d10 damage

    Heightened

    For each +1 Mana, the damage increases by 1d10.

    Elemental Shield Novice

    Specializations

    Air

    You gain an additional +8 Defense against ranged Strikes.

    Earth

    You gain 10 Physical Shield.

    Fire

    If your attacker is adjacent to you, they take 2d6 Fire damage.

    Water

    If the triggering attack critically fails against you, it targets your attacker instead (and is automatically a success).

      Specialize Spell
    Cost
    1 Mana
    Trigger
    An attack targets your Defense
    Duration
    Until the start of your next turn

    You gain +2 Defense.

    Freeze Novice

      Attack Cold Spell
    Cost
    1 Mana
    Area
    30-foot cone
    Duration
    Varies

    Make a Magic attack roll against your target’s Agility DC. You have advantage on the attack roll if your target is partially surmerged in water.

    Success
    Your target takes 2d8 damage and is restrained for 1 minute
    Failure
    Half damage, and your target is slowed until the end of their next turn

    Heightened

    For each +1 Mana, the damage increases by 1d8.

    Avatar Veteran

    Prerequisites
    Any Specialize Elementalist power

    Choose one Specialize Elementalist power you know. Change the specialization you picked for that power. If the power is currently active, this doesn’t apply to that instance of the power.

    Elemental Flight Veteran

    Specializations

    Air

    The wind lifts your target into the sky.

    Your target’s Speed is doubled.

    Earth

    Pillars of earth launch your target through the air.

    Your target can only fly during their turn, but they are immune to fall damage. Your target can burrow at their normal Speed.

    Fire

    Jets of fire propel your target forward.

    Your target has +2 Agility DC.

    Water

    A spiral of water lifts your target off the ground.

    Your target can only fly within 30 feet of a solid or liquid surface. Your target can swim at their normal Speed.

    Ribbons

    Create Water Ribbon

      Cantrip Spell
    Cost
    0 Mana
    Range
    5 feet

    You conjure a jet of water that arcs towards your target. The water is pure and safe to drink. This spell creates a total of 1 gallon of water.

    Heightened

    For each +1 Mana (up to +6 Mana), this power’s range extends by 10 feet, and you create 10 times as much water.

    Feather Fall Ribbon

      Spell
    Cost
    1 Mana
    Trigger
    You see a creature fall
    Range
    60 feet
    Target
    Up to three falling creatures you can see within range
    Duration
    1 minute, or until a target stops falling

    Your target slows down in midair, falling only 60 feet each round, and they are immune to fall damage.

    Heightened

    For each +1 Mana, you can target one additional creature.

    Tinder Ribbon

      Cantrip Fire Spell
    Cost
    0 Mana
    Duration
    Sustained, up to 1 minute

    You conjure a small flame at your fingertip or in your hand. The flame doesn’t require fuel and doesn’t burn you. You can touch an object to rapidly heat it up or to set fire to a flammable object. The flame isn’t large enough to be effective as an attack.

    Heightened

    If you add +1 Mana, this power gains a range of 30 feet, and targets an unattended object you can see within range. The flame does burn your target, quickly setting fire to a flammable target. If you add an additional +1 Mana, this power gains a range of 60 feet, gains an area of a 20-foot burst, and targets all unattended objects in the area.

    Tremorsense Ribbon

    Range
    30 feet

    You perceive all creatures and objects within range that you are connected to by a path of a solid material. If this material is entirely hard earth or stone, then your sense is as accurate as vision or touch. Otherwise, your sense is less accurate, and you have disadvantage on rolls that depend on perceiving a target. Your sense doesn’t work at all through soft materials, such as sand or water.
