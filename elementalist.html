    Concealing Fog Novice

      Ritual Spell
    Cost
    1 Mana
    Range
    120 feet
    Area
    20-foot burst
    Duration
    1 hour, or until you decide to end it

    You conjure a cloud of dense fog that fills the area. The fog completely obscures vision, making sight impossible and nullifying Visual effects. The fog partially obscures sound, imposing disadvantage on Auditory attacks and die rolls to hear things within the area.

    Heightened (per +1 Mana)
    The radius of the burst increases by 20 feet

    Elemental Bolt Novice

      Attack Cantrip Spell
    Cost
    0 Mana
    Range
    120 feet

    Make a Magic attack roll against your target’s defense. On a success, your target takes 1d8 damage.

    Heightened (per +1 Mana)
    The damage increases by 1d8

    Earth Spike Novice

      Attack Spell
    Cost
    1 Mana
    Range
    60 feet

    Make a Magic attack roll against your target’s Agility DC. On a success, your target takes 3d6 damage and falls prone. On a failure, your target takes half damage. In either case, the ground in a 10-foot burst around your target becomes difficult terrain, as spikes or pillars of earth persist on the landscape.

    Heightened (total 3 Mana)
    This power gains an area of a 10-foot burst, and the damage is doubled against structures
    Heightened (total 5 Mana)
    The damage increases to 5d6, and the area increases to a 20-foot burst

    Create Water Ribbon

      Cantrip Spell
    Cost
    0 Mana
    Range
    5 feet

    You conjure a jet of water that arcs towards your target. The water is pure and safe to drink. One casting of this spell creates a gallon of water.

    Heightened (per +1 Mana, up to a maximum of +6 Mana)
    This power’s range extends by 10 feet, and you create 10 times as much water

    Freeze Ribbon

      Cantrip Spell
    Cost
    0 Mana
    Area
    30-foot line

    All substances in the area are cooled towards near freezing temperatures. This puts out small fires and freezes small bodies of water. This effect isn’t powerful enough to affect large objects, creatures, or magical heat sources.

    Heightened (total 2 Mana)
    This power gains the Attack trait and targets all creatures in the area that are partially submerged in water. Make a Magic attack roll against your target’s Agility DC. On a success, your target takes 2d8 damage and is restrained until the end of your next turn. On a failure, your target takes 1d8 damage and is slowed until the end of your next turn.

    Tinder Ribbon

      Cantrip Spell
    Cost
    0 Mana
    Duration
    Sustained, up to 1 minute

    You conjure a small flame at your fingertip or in your hand. The flame doesn’t require fuel and doesn’t burn you. You can touch an object to rapidly heat it up or to set fire to a flammable object. The flame isn’t large enough to be effective as an attack.

    Heightened (total 1 Mana)
    This power gains a range of 30 feet, and targets an unattended object you can see within range. The flame does burn your target, quickly setting fire to a flammable target.
    Heightened (total 2 Mana)
    This power gains a range of 60 feet, gains an area of a 20-foot burst, and targets all unattended objects in the area
