    Heal Novice

      Spell
    Cost
    1 Mana
    Range
    Touch

    Your target heals 5 health and is cured of any nonmagical diseases. If your target is an undead, they instead take 1d6 damage.

    Heightened (per +1 Mana)
    The healing increases by 5 and the damage increases by 1d6

    Smite Evil Novice

      Spell
    Cost
    1 Mana
    Trigger
    You hit a creature with a Strike
    Target
    The creature you hit

    Your target takes 1d6 damage. If your target is an undead or fiend, this damage is doubled.

    Heightened (per +1 Mana)
    The damage increases by 1d6

    Sacred Spell Veteran

      Encounter
    Trigger
    You cast a spell

    Reduce the cost of the spell by 2.

    Holy Aura Ribbon

    You constantly emit a strong magical aura. The nature of the aura matches your beliefs - if you follow good beliefs and constantly help others, you emit an aura of Good, and vice-versa for Evil. Some creatures, including undead, angels, and demons, can sense holy auras.

    Light Ribbon

      Cantrip Spell
    Cost
    0 Mana
    Target
    An unattended object you can reach
    Duration
    1 hour

    Your target shines bright light in all directions. The light is as bright as a torch, illuminating the area within 30 feet.

    Heightened (per +1 Mana)
    The radius of illumination increases by 30 feet

    Zone of Truth Ribbon

      Ritual Spell
    Cost
    2 Mana
    Area
    30-foot burst
    Duration
    10 minutes

    Whenever anyone in the area knowingly speaks a lie, the spell sends you a ping. You choose whether the ping is perceptible to only you or to everyone nearby.
