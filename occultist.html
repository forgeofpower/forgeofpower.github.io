    Occultist

    TODO - actually write a description.

    When you reach Novice tier in this power school, you gain an arcane background if you don’t have one already.

    Powers

    Name Tier Type Description
    Aid Novice Bolster your allies’ toughness and resolve
    Bless Novice Bless your allies with good fortune and protection
    Guiding Bolt Novice Strike an enemy and open them up to further attacks
    Healing Word Novice Quickly heal an ally to get them back on their feet
    Haste Veteran Boost an ally’s speed, letting them move and act quickly
    Blessed Healer Ribbon Healing spells you cast on others heal you as well
    Gentle Affect Ribbon You appear generally peaceful and nonthreatening to others
    Sanctuary Ribbon You’re hard to hurt while you don’t attack your enemies
    Shield of Faith Ribbon Conjure a magical field to protect your ally
    Supreme Healer Ribbon Reroll the dice on one healing spell

    Aid Novice

      Spell
    Cost
    2 Mana
    Range
    30 feet
    Target
    Up to three creatures you can see within range
    Duration
    8 hours

    Your targets’ maximum and current health both increase by 5.

    Heightened (per +1 Mana)
    You targets’ health increases by an additional 5

    Bless Novice

      Spell
    Cost
    1 Mana
    Range
    30 feet
    Target
    Up to three creatures you can see within range
    Duration
    Sustained, up to 1 minute

    Whenever your target makes an attack roll, they can add 1d4 to their result. Whenever they are the target of an attack, they can add 1d4 to the DC of the attack against them.

    Heightened (per +1 Mana)
    You target one additional creature

    Guiding Bolt Novice

      Attack Cantrip Spell
    Cost
    0 Mana
    Range
    120 feet
    Duration
    1 minute

    Make a Magic attack roll against your target’s defense. On a success, your target takes 1d6 damage. Whether or not your attack succeeds, your target shines with a dim glittering light, and the next attack roll against your target has advantage.

    Heightened (per +1 Mana)
    The damage increases by 1d6

    Healing Word Novice

      Spell
    Cost
    1 Mana
    Range
    60 feet

    Your target heals 1d6 health.

    Heightened (per +1 Mana)
    Your target heals an additional 1d6 health

    Haste Veteran

      Spell
    Cost
    3 Mana
    Range
    Touch
    Duration
    Sustained, up to 1 minute

    Your target gains the Hasted Action power.

    Hasted Action
    Use a basic power of your choice.

    Blessed Healer Ribbon

    When you spend at least 1 Mana on a spell that heals an ally, you heal health equal to the Mana you spent.

    Gentle Affect Ribbon

    Other people intially judge you as gentle, peaceful, and kindly. This usually makes people more likely to trust and like you, and less likely to believe you’re capable of violence. This impression can be changed by your actions or by a conflicting reputation.

    Sanctuary Ribbon

    You have a +2 bonus to defense and to your Spirit DC. If you use any Attack power, you lose this benefit for the next hour.

    Shield of Faith Ribbon

      Spell
    Cost
    1 Mana
    Range
    60 feet
    Duration
    10 minutes

    Your target has a +2 bonus to defense.

    Supreme Healer Ribbon

      Daily
    Trigger
    You heal a creature with a spell

    Reroll the amount of healing. You pick whether to use the original result or the new one.
