    Befriend Animal Novice

      Attack Auditory Spell
    Cost
    1 Mana
    Target
    An animal you can reach that has CR no higher than your character level
    Duration
    24 hours, until you choose to end it, or until you or one of your companions harms the target

    Make a Magic attack roll against your target’s Will. If your target is already friendly towards you, this attack automatically succeeds. On a success, your target becomes friendly and loyal to you, gaining the Minion trait. Your target obeys any command you give it, even putting itself at risk, unless those directions are obviously suicidal.

    Entangle Novice

      Attack Spell
    Cost
    1 Mana
    Range
    60 feet
    Area
    All creatures on the ground in a 20-foot burst
    Duration
    1 minute

    Make a Magic attack roll against your target’s Agility DC. On a success, your target is immobilized. On a critical success, your target is also restrained. Creatures can use the Escape power to end these effects.

    Goodberry Novice

      Spell
    Cost
    1 Mana
    Duration
    1 hour

    Up to 10 berries appear in your hand. A creature can eat a berry (or feed it to an ally) with the Interact power. Eating a berry heals 1 health and provides enough nourishment to sustain a normal human for one day. Any uneaten berries disappear when the duration ends.

    Magic Acorns Novice

      Cantrip Spell
    Cost
    0 Mana
    Target
    Up to 5 acorns, nuts, pinecones, seeds, or similar objects you can reach
    Duration
    1 minute

    The targets are magically enhanced to act as amazing projectiles. These projectiles count as ranged weapons with a damage of 1d8 and a range of 60.

    Heightened (total 1 Mana)
    All the projectiles gain one of the following effects.

    Fiery. The projectiles explode with a burst of flame when they hit. On a succesful Strike, all creatures in a 5-foot burst around the target take 1d6 damage.

    Slowing. The projectiles cover their target in sticky sap. On a successful Strike, the target’s Speed is reduced by 10 feet until the end of its next turn.

    Thorn Whip Novice

      Attack Cantrip Spell
    Cost
    0 Mana
    Range
    30 feet

    Make a Magic attack roll against your target’s Agility DC. On a success, your target takes 2d6 damage.

    Heightened (total 2 Mana)
    The damage increases to 4d6. On a success, if your target is Large or smaller, they are pulled up to 10 feet closer to you and are immobilized until the start of your next turn.

    Detect Poison Ribbon

      Ritual Spell
    Cost
    1 Mana
    Area
    30-foot burst

    You detect all poisons, poisonous creatures, diseases, and diseased creatures in the area. You sense the precise location and the exact type of poison or disease.

    Friend of Nature Ribbon

    Animals and intelligent plants tend to like you. Unless given reason to act otherwise, they will treat you as a trusted friend or ally. If you have the Speak with Animals power, you can usually get animals to perform simple favors for you.

    Land's Stride Ribbon

    Moving through nonmagical difficult terrain costs you no extra movement. You can also pass through nonmagical plants without being slowed by them and without taking damage from them if they have thorns, spines, or a similar hazard.

    Spark of Growth Ribbon

      Cantrip Ritual Spell
    Cost
    0 Mana
    Target
    A nonmagical plant you can reach

    Your target instantly undergoes up to a year of normal growth. The plant might grow in size, gain or shed leaves, or grow blossoms. Your target is then immune to this power for the next week.

    Heightened (per +1 Mana)
    Your target heals 1d6 health or recovers from one disease afflicting it

    Speak with Animals Ribbon

      Auditory

    You can let animals can understand your speech, no matter what language you speak in. In turn, you can decipher an animal’s intent by interpreting its noises and motions. Most animals lack the intelligence to understand sophisticated concepts.
