    Talents

    Befriend Animal Novice

      Attack Auditory Spell
    Cost
    1 Mana
    Target
    A Beast creature you can reach that has CR no higher than your character level
    Duration
    24 hours, until you decide to end it, or until you or one of your companions harms the target

    Make a Magic attack roll against your target’s Spirit DC. If your target is already friendly towards you, this attack automatically succeeds.

    Success
    Your target becomes totally loyal to you and gains the Minion trait. Your target obeys any command you give it, even putting itself at risk, unless those directions are obviously suicidal.

    Entangle Novice

      Attack Spell
    Cost
    1 Mana
    Range
    60 feet
    Area
    20-foot burst
    Requirements
    Your target is on the ground
    Duration
    Forever

    Make a Magic attack roll against your target’s Agility DC

    Critical Success
    Your target is restrained
    Success
    Your target is immobilized
    Failure
    Your target is slowed until the end of its next turn

    Goodberry Novice

      Healing Spell
    Cost
    1 Mana
    Duration
    1 hour

    Up to 10 berries appear in your hand. A creature can eat a berry (or feed it to an ally) with the Interact power. Eating a berry heals 1 health and provides enough nourishment to sustain a normal human for one day. Any uneaten berries disappear when the duration ends.

    Magic Acorns Novice

      Cantrip Spell
    Cost
    0 Mana
    Target
    Up to 5 acorns, nuts, pinecones, seeds, or similar objects you can reach
    Duration
    1 minute

    The targets are magically enhanced to act as amazing projectiles. These projectiles count as ranged weapons with a base damage of 1d8 Bludgeoning and a range of 60 feet.

    Heightened

    If you add +1 Mana, all the projectiles gain one of the following effects.

    Fiery. The projectiles explode with a burst of flame when they hit. On a successful Strike, all creatures in a 5-foot burst around the target take 1d6 Fire damage.

    Slowing. The projectiles cover their target in sticky sap. On a successful Strike, the target is slowed until the end of its next turn.

    Thorn Whip Novice

      Attack Cantrip Piercing Spell
    Cost
    0 Mana
    Range
    30 feet

    Make a Magic attack roll against your target’s Agility DC.

    Critical Success
    Double damage
    Success
    Your target takes 2d6 damage and is pulled up to 10 feet towards you

    Heightened

    For each +1 Mana, the damage increases by 1d6.

    Awaken Veteran

      Ritual Spell
    Cost
    5 Mana
    Target
    One Beast creature you can reach

    Your target becomes sentient, gaining intelligence as an Awakened. Your target’s form morphs to become more humanoid: they gain the proper limbs, can breathe air, gain opposable thumbs, can speak humanoid language, and so on.

    After the transformation, your target is usually friendly towards you, though they may be shocked by the sudden change. If your target was loyal to you before the transformation, they act as a Minion loyal to you for the next hour, after which they become a normal NPC.

    Instant Tree Veteran

      Attack Bludgeoning Spell
    Cost
    3 Mana
    Range
    60 feet
    Area
    20-foot line

    A tree grows almost instantly, starting from the specified point, to fill the spell’s area. You choose the species of tree. Once the tree is done growing, it stays around forever as a normal tree.

    Make a Magic attack roll against your target’s Agility DC.

    Critical Success
    Double damage and double push distance
    Success
    Your target takes 4d10 damage and is pushed 20 feet away from the base of the tree
    Failure
    Half damage

    Heightened

    For each +1 Mana, the damage increases by 1d10, the area extends by 10 feet, and the push distance increases by 10 feet.

    We can never forget the Treeagle incident of 2015.

    Ribbons

    Detect Poison Ribbon

      Ritual Spell
    Cost
    1 Mana
    Area
    30-foot burst

    You detect all poisons, poisonous creatures, diseases, and diseased creatures in the area. You sense the precise location and the exact type of poison or disease.

    Friend of Nature Ribbon

    Animals and intelligent plants tend to like you. Unless given reason to act otherwise, they will treat you as a trusted friend or ally. If you have the Speak with Animals power, you can usually get animals to perform simple favors for you.

    Land's Stride Ribbon

    Moving through nonmagical difficult terrain costs you no extra movement. You can also pass through nonmagical plants without being slowed by them and without taking damage from them if they have thorns, spines, or a similar hazard.

    Spark of Growth Ribbon

      Cantrip Healing Ritual Spell
    Cost
    0 Mana
    Target
    A nonmagical plant you can reach
    Requirements
    Your target hasn’t been affected by Spark of Growth in the past week

    Your target instantly undergoes up to a year of normal growth. The plant might grow in size, gain or shed leaves, or grow blossoms.

    Heightened

    For each +1 Mana, your target heals 1d6 health or recovers from one disease afflicting it.

    Speak with Animals Ribbon

      Auditory

    You can let animals can understand your speech, no matter what language you speak in. In turn, you can decipher an animal’s intent by interpreting its noises and motions. Most animals lack the intelligence to understand sophisticated concepts.
