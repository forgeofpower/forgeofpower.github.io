      Incapacitation Spell Visual
    Cost
    1 Mana
    Area
    15-foot cone
    Save
    Will
    Duration
    1 or more rounds

    Swirling colors affect viewers based on their Will saves.

    Critical Success
    The creature is unaffected
    Success
    The creature is dazzled for 1 round
    Failure
    The creature is stunned 1, blinded for 1 round, and dazzled for 1 minute
    Critical Failure
    The creature is stunned for 1 round and blinded for 1 minute

    Illusory Disguise Novice

      Spell Visual
    Cost
    1 Mana
    Duration
    1 hour

    You create an illusion that causes you to appear as another creature of the same body shape, and with roughly similar height and weight as yourself. The disguise is typically good enough to hide your identity, but not to impersonate a specific individual. The spell doesn’t change your voice, scent, or mannerisms. You can change the appearance of your clothing and worn items, but held items are unaffected, and any worn item you remove returns to its true appearance.

    Heightened (total 2 Mana)
    The spell also disguises your voice and scent, and it gains the Auditory trait
    Heightened (total 3 Mana)
    You can appear as any creature of the same size, even a specific individual as long as you’ve seen them before
    Heightened (total 6 Mana)
    You can target up to 10 creatures within 30 feet

    Illusory Object Novice

      Visual Spell
    Cost
    1 Mana
    Range
    500 feet
    Area
    20-foot burst
    Duration
    10 minutes

    You create an illusory visual image of a stationary object. The entire image must fit within the spell’s area. The object appears to animate naturally, but it doesn’t make sounds or generate smells. Any creature that touches the image or uses the Seek action to examine it can attempt to disbelieve your illusion.

    Heightened (total 2 Mana)
    Your image makes appropriate sounds, generates normal smells, and feels right to the touch. The spell gains the Auditory trait. The duration increases to 1 hour.
    Heightened (total 5 Mana)
    The duration is unlimited.

    Invisibility Novice

      Spell
    Cost
    2 Mana
    Range
    Touch
    Duration
    10 minutes

    Cloaked in illusion, the target becomes invisible. This makes it hidden from all creatures that rely on sight. If the target uses a hostile action, the spell ends after that action is completed.

    Heightened (total 4 Mana)
    The spell lasts 1 minute, but it doesn’t end if the target uses a hostile action

    Phantom Pain Novice

      Mental Spell
    Cost
    1 Mana
    Range
    30 feet
    Save
    Will
    Duration
    1 minute

    Illusory pain wracks the target, dealing 2d4 mental damage and 1d4 persistent mental damage. The target must attempt a Will save.

    Critical Success
    The target is unaffected
    Success
    The target takes full initial damage but no persistent damage, and the spell ends immediately
    Failure
    The target takes full initial and persistent damage, and the target is sickened 1
    Critical Failure
    As failure, but the target is sickened 2

    Illusory Scene Veteran

    Prerequisites
    Illusory Object

    Your Illusory Object power can form a scene that includes up to 10 moving creatures or objects. You can have each part of the scene follow a repeating program up to 1 minute long. A creature that interacts with one part of your image only disbelieves that portion.

    Phantasmal Killer Veteran

      Death Emotion Fear Mental Spell
    Cost
    4 Mana
    Range
    120 feet
    Save
    Will

    You create a phantasmal image of the most fearsome creature imaginable to the target. Only the spell’s target can see the killer, though you can see the vague shape of the illusion as it races forth to attack. The effect of the killer is based on the outcome of the target’s Will save.

    Heightened (per +1 Mana)
    The damage increases by 1d6 on a success, by 2d6 on a failure, and by 3d6 on a critical failure
    Critical Success
    The target is unaffected
    Success
    The target takes 4d6 mental damage and is frightened 1
    Failure
    The target takes 8d6 mental damage and is frightened 2
    Critical Failure
    The target is so afraid it might die. It must attempt a Fortitude save (with the Incapacitation trait); if the target fails, it dies. On a successful Fortitude save, the target takes 12d6 mental damage, is fleeing until the end of its next turn, and is frightened 4.

    Shadow Siphon Veteran

      Shadow Spell
    Cost
    5 Mana
    Range
    60 feet
    Trigger
    A spell or magical effect within range deals damage

    Exchanging material energy with shadows, you transform the triggering spell into a partially illusory version of itself. Any creatures that would be damaged by the spell instead take only half as much damage, but the spell otherwise works as normal.

    Project Image Master

      Mental Spell
    Cost
    7 Mana
    Range
    30 feet
    Duration
    Sustained up to 1 minute

    You project an illusory image of yourself. You must stay within range of the image, and if at any point you can’t see the image, the spell ends. Whenever you cast a spell, you can cause the spell effect to originate from either yourself or the image. Because the image is an illusion, it isn’t a creature and can’t benefit from spells. The image has the same AC and saves as you. If it is hit by an attack or fails a save, the spell ends.

    Heightened (total 9 Mana)
    The maximum duration you can Sustan the spell increases to 10 minutes

    Weird Epic

    Prerequisites
    Phantasmal Killer

    When you spend at least 8 Mana on Phantasmal Killer, the spell targets any number of creatures within range.

    Command Novice

      Auditory Linguistic Mental Spell
    Cost
    1 Mana
    Range
    30 feet
    Save
    Will
    Duration
    Until the end of the target’s next turn

    You shout a command that’s hard to ignore. You can command the target to approach you, run away (as if it had the fleeing condition), release what it’s holding, drop prone, or stand in place. It can’t Delay or take any reactions until it has obeyed your command. The effects depend on the target’s Will save.

    Heightened (total 5 Mana)
    You can target up to 10 creatures
    Success
    The creature is unaffected
    Failure
    For the first action on its next turn, the creature must use a single action to do as you command
    Critical Failure
    The target must use all its actions on its next turn to obey your command

    Confusion Veteran

      Emotion Mental Spell
    Cost
    4 Mana
    Range
    30 feet
    Save
    Will

    You befuddle your target with strange impulses, causing it to act randomly. The effects are determined by the target’s Will save. You can Dismiss the spell.

    Heightened (total 8 Mana)
    You can target up to 10 creatures
    Critical Success
    The target is unaffected
    Success
    The target babbles incoherently and is stunned 1
    Failure
    The target is confused for 1 minute, and can attempt a new save at the end of each of its turns to end the confusion
    Critical Failure
    The target is confused for 1 minute, with no save to end the effect early

    Mind Reading Veteran

      Detection Mental Spell
    Cost
    3 Mana
    Range
    30 feet
    Save
    Will
    Duration
    1 round, or sustained up to 1 minute

    With a cursory mental touch, you attempt to read the target’s mind. It must attempt a Will save. The target then becomes temporarily immune to your Mind Reading power for 1 hour.

    Critical Success
    The target perceives vague surface thoughts from you
    Success
    You find out whether the target’s Intelligence is higher than, equal to, or lower than yours
    Failure
    You perceive vague surface thoughts from the target, and you find out whether its Intelligence is higher than, equal to, or lower than yours
    Critical Failure
    As failure, and for the duration of the spell, you can Sustain to detect the target’s surface throughts again

    Suggestion Veteran

      Incapacitation Linguistic Mental Spell
    Cost
    4 Mana
    Range
    30 feet
    Save
    Will
    Duration
    Varies

    Your honeyed words are difficult for creatures to resist. You suggest a course of action to the target, which must be phrased in such a way as to seem like a logical course of action to the target and can’t be self-destructive or obviously against the target’s self-interest. The target must attempt a Will save.

    Heightened (total 5 Mana)
    You can implant the suggestion in the target’s subconscious, waiting for a trigger you specify. The first time the trigger occurs during the next 24 hours, the target immediately follows your suggestion.
    Heightened (total 8 Mana)
    You can target up to 10 creatures
    Critical Success
    The target is unaffected and knows you tried to control it
    Success
    The target is unaffected and thinks you were talking to them normally, not casting a spell on them
    Failure
    The target immediately follows your suggestion. The spell has a duration of 1 minute, or until the target has completeda a finite suggestion or the suggestion becomes self-destructive or has other obvious negative effects.
    Critical Failure
    As failure, but the base duration is 1 hour

    Dominate Master

      Incapacitation Mental Spell
    Cost
    6 Mana
    Range
    30 feet
    Save
    Will
    Duration
    24 hours

    You take command of the target, forcing it to obey your orders. If you issue an obviously self-destructive order, the target doesn’t act until you issue a new order. The effect depends on its Will save.

    Heightened (total 10 Mana)
    The duration is unlimited
    Critical Success
    The target is unaffected
    Success
    The target is stunned 1 as it fights off your commands
    Failure
    The target follows your orders but can attempt a Will save at the end of each of its turns. On a success, the spell ends.
    Critical Failure
    As a failure, but the target receives a new save only when you give it a new order that is against its nature, such as killing its allies.

    Warp Mind Master

      Emotion Incapacitation Mental Spell
    Cost
    7 Mana
    Range
    120 feet
    Save
    Will

    You scramble a creature’s mental faculties and sensory input. The target must attempt a Will saving throw. Regardless of the result of that save, the target is then temporarily immune for 10 minutes.

    Critical Success
    The target is unaffected
    Success
    The target spends the first action on its next turn with the confused condition
    Failure
    The target is confused for 1 minute
    Critical Failure
    The target is confused permanently

    Fabricated Truth Epic

      Incapacitation Mental Spell
    Cost
    10 Mana
    Range
    100 feet
    Targets
    Up to 5 creatures
    Save
    Will
    Duration
    Varies

    Choose a single statement you want the targets to believe. The fact can be narrow, wide-reaching, or conceptual, and can be completely unreasonable or ridiculous. If the statement changes what a target perceives, they treat the change as a sudden revelation. The effect of the spell depends on the targets’ Will saves.

    Critical Success
    The target doesn’t believe the statement, and it knows you tried to trick it
    Success
    The target doesn’t believe the statement or realize you tried to trick it
    Failure
    The target believes the statement for a duration of 1 week
    Critical Failure
    The target believes the statement with unlimited duration
