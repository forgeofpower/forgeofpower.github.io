    Illusionist

    Illusionists are tricky mages who deceive enemies with clever illusions. Illusionists never fight directly, but instead manipulate other creatures’ senses to make them see things that aren’t there or overlook things that are. Many illusionists work as performers, artists, or technical crew, as their magic opens up new possibilities in the performing arts.

    Illusionist talents include a variety of ways to distract or disable enemies. Many illusionist talents force enemies to waste time attacking things that aren’t really there. Others manipulate creatures’ minds directly, causing confusing or inciting strong emotions. Illusionist ribbons are mostly small-scale illusions useful out of combat.

    Examples of illusionists in fiction include Loki from Marvel Comics, Genjutsu-users from Naruto, and almost every magic-user in real-world mythology.

    Illusory Clone Novice

      Illusion Spell
    Cost
    1 Mana
    Duration
    1 hour, until the clone falls to 0 health, or until you decide to end it

    You conjure a visual illusion that appears as an exact clone of yourself. Your clone is a Minion creature that you control. Your clone has all the same stats as you, except its Health is equal to your Illusionist level and it shares your Mana pool. Since your clone is an illusion, it cannot physically interact with the world (unless you have the Tactile Illusions talent). Your clone cannot use any powers other than Stride.

    You have a telepathic link with your clone that lets you communicate instantly and share each others’ senses: you can see through each others’ eyes and feel everything the other feels. Your clone is perfectly loyal to you and obeys all your commands.

    Your clone has duplicates of all your equipment. However, duplicated magic items lose their magic, and all duplicated equipment fades a few seconds after it is no longer touching your clone.

    Heightened

    If you add +2 Mana, your clone is a real creature made of magical shadow. Your clone is no longer an illusion, and this spell loses the Illusion trait. Your clone can physically interact with the world, and it can use any power you can. If your clone uses an Encounter or Daily power, that power counts as expended for you, and vice versa. Your clone has disadvantage on all attack rolls.

    For each +1 Mana, you conjure an additional clone.

    Illusory Disguise Novice

      Illusion Spell
    Cost
    1 Mana
    Range
    10 feet
    Duration
    1 hour, or until you decide to end it

    Your target is covered in a visual illusion that makes them look like another creature of the same body shape, and with roughly similar height and weight as themself. The illusion can change the appearance of your target’s clothing and worn items, but held items are unaffected, and any worn item your target removes returns to its true appearance.

    Heightened

    For each +1 Mana, you can target another creature within range.

    1 Mana
    Range
    500 feet
    Area
    20-foot burst
    Duration
    10 minutes, or until you decide to end it

    You create an illusory visual image of a stationary object or structure. The entire image must fit within the spell’s area.

    Heightened

    If you add +1 Mana, this spell’s duration changes to 24 hours. If you instead add +3 Mana, this spell’s duration changes to Forever.

    Invisibility Novice

    Invisible Condition

    If you are invisible, you cannot be seen at all. This is enough to completely hide you from anyone who is not actively searching for you. Other creatures can still try to determine your location through sound clues, puffs of dust, or odd breezes. Creatures have disadvantage on Intellect+Notice rolls made to find you.

      Spell Visual

    Heightened

    For each +1 Mana, you can target another creature within range.

    Tactile Illusions Novice

    Prerequisites
    Any Illusion power

    Your illusions form actual solid objects capable of supporting weight. Your illusions make appropriate sounds, generate normal smells, and feel right to the touch. Your Illusion powers gain the Auditory and Tactile traits.

    Your illusions are moderately solid, but don’t stand up to any significant force. Illusions you make lose their solidity if they take any damage. If you conjure a solid illusion on top of another solid object, your illusion loses its solidity immediately.

    3 Mana

    Ribbons

    Chameleon Ribbon

      Cantrip Illusion Spell
    Cost
    0 Mana
    Duration
    1 hour

    You conjure an illusion that changes a minor part of your visual appearance. For example, you could change your eye color, your hairstyle, or the pattern on a tattoo. This change isn’t large enough to disguise your identity.

    Glamour Ribbon

      Illusion

    You are covered by an illusion that makes you appear striking, glamorous, or beautiful. You have advantage on Spirit+Perform rolls that rely on your visual apperance.

    Minor Illusion Ribbon

      Cantrip Illusion Spell
    Cost
    0 Mana
    Range
    30 feet
    Area
    5-foot cube

    You create an illusory visual image of a stationary object or structure. The entire image must fit within the spell’s area.

    Alternatively, you conjure a sound originating from the spell’s area. This power loses the Illusion trait and gains the Auditory trait.
