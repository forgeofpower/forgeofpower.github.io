    Healer

    Powers

    Name Tier Type Description
    Aid Novice You magically bolster your allies’ toughness and resolve
    Bless Novice You bless your allies with good fortune and protection
    Guiding Bolt Novice You shoot a bolt of light at an enemy, highlighting their weak points
    Heal Novice You restore a creature’s health and cure diseases
    Smite Evil Novice You punish an evil target with powerful radiant energy
    Sacred Spell Veteran You channel divine energy to greatly buff a spell you cast
    Blessed Healer Ribbon Healing spells you cast on others heal you as well
    Gentle Affect Ribbon You appear generally peaceful and nonthreatening to others
    Light Ribbon You cause an object to shine bright light like a torch
    Priestly Status Ribbon You’re known as an officially-sanctioned priest of a common religion
    Sanctuary Ribbon You’re hard to hurt while you don’t attack your enemies

    Talents

    Aid Novice

      Spell
    Cost
    2 Mana
    Range
    30 feet
    Target
    Up to three creatures you can see within range
    Duration
    8 hours

    Your target’s maximum and current health both increase by 2d6.

    Heightened

    For each +1 Mana, your target’s maximum and current health both increase by an additional 1d6.

    Bless Novice

      Spell
    Cost
    1 Mana
    Range
    30 feet
    Target
    Up to three creatures you can see within range
    Duration
    Sustained, up to 1 minute

    Whenever your target makes an attack roll, they can add 1d4 to their result. Whenever they are the target of an attack, they can add 1d4 to their DC against the attack.

    Heightened

    For each +1 Mana, you target one additional creature.

    Guiding Bolt Novice

      Attack Cantrip Light Radiant Spell
    Cost
    0 Mana
    Range
    120 feet
    Duration
    1 minute

    Make a Magic attack roll against your target’s Defense.

    Success
    Your target takes 1d6 damage

    Whether or not this attack succeeds, your target glitters with light, shining dim light out to 10 feet. The next attack roll against your target has advantage.

    Heightened

    For each +1 Mana, the damage increases by 2d6.

    Heal Novice

      Healing Spell
    Cost
    1 Mana
    Range
    Touch

    Your target heals 5 health and is cured of any nonmagical diseases. If your target is an Undead or an Outsider, they instead take 4d6 damage.

    Heightened

    For each +1 Mana, the healing increases by 5 and the damage increases by 2d6.

    Smite Evil Novice

      Radiant Spell
    Cost
    1 Mana
    Trigger
    You succeed on a melee Strike
    Target
    The target of your Strike

    Your target takes 2d6 damage. If your target is an Undead or an Outsider, this damage is doubled.

    Heightened

    For each +1 Mana, the damage increases by 2d6.

    Sacred Spell Veteran

      Encounter
    Trigger
    You cast a spell

    Reduce the cost of the triggering spell by 2.

    Ribbons

    Blessed Healer Ribbon

    When you spend at least 1 Mana on a Healing spell, you heal health equal to the Mana you spent.

    Gentle Affect Ribbon

      Reputation

    Other people intially judge you as gentle, peaceful, and kindly. This usually makes people more likely to trust and like you, and less likely to believe you’re capable of violence.

    Light Ribbon

      Cantrip Light Spell
    Cost
    0 Mana
    Target
    An unattended object you can reach
    Duration
    8 hours

    Your target shines bright light in all directions. The light is as bright as a torch, illuminating the area within 30 feet.

    Heightened

    For each +1 Mana, the radius of illumination increases by 30 feet.

    Priestly Status Ribbon

      Reputation

    You are widely known as a priest of a particular religion. Followers of that religion generally respect your moral and religious authority.

    Sanctuary Ribbon

    You have a +2 bonus to Defense and to your Spirit DC. If you use any Attack power, you lose this benefit for the next hour.
